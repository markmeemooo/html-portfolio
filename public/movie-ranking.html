The Best Movies According to Mark

These are my 3 favourite movies.

Memento

One of my favourite Christopher Nolan movies with a nice twist at the end.

Mononoke Hime

One of my first Hayao Miyazaki movies. Great story, awesome soundtrack, wolves.

Spiderman

Not the best film in terms of storytelling or quality but I distinctly remember this being one of my favourite movie theater experiences ever.
